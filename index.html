MEAT MEAL

Meat your cravings in every bite!

Filipino meat dishes are a must-try for any food lover. From the tangy flavors of adobo to the

crispy texture of lechon, these dishes are sure to leave you satisfied and craving for more.

Don't miss out on the opportunity to taste the unique and delicious flavors of Filipino cuisine.

  • Humba

    made with pork belly that are simmered in a sauce made with soy sauce, vinegar, brown sugar, garlic, and other spices.

  • Pork Adobo

    a flavorful dish made with meat (usually chicken or pork) marinated in vinegar, soy sauce, and garlic.

  • Sisig

    a sizzling pork dish made with chopped pig's head and liver, seasoned with calamansi and chili peppers.

  • Crispy Pata

    a deep-fried pork knuckle that's crispy on the outside and tender on the inside.

  • Mechado

    a beef stew made with tomato sauce, soy sauce, and various spices.

  • Bistek

    a beef dish marinated in soy sauce and calamansi, topped with caramelized onions.

  • Sinigang

    a sour soup dish made with meat (usually pork or beef) and vegetables such as tamarind, tomato, and onion.

  • Lechon

    a whole roasted pig that is a staple in Filipino celebrations.

  • Chicken Afritada

    chicken stewed in a tomato-based sauce with vegetables such as potatoes, carrots, and bell peppers.
